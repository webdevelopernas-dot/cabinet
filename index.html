About Us

Crafting Excellence Since 2009

M&N Modular Cabinet Installer has been at the forefront of custom cabinetry, transforming homes and commercial spaces with precision-engineered modular solutions. Our journey began with a simple mission: to make luxury cabinetry accessible without compromising on quality.

Today, we are recognized as industry leaders, combining traditional woodworking artistry with modern CNC technology. Our team of 45+ skilled craftsmen and designers work collaboratively to deliver projects that exceed expectations.

Installation timelines vary by project scope. A standard kitchen takes 3-5 days, while a full home project may take 2-3 weeks. We provide detailed timelines during consultation and always strive to meet deadlines without compromising quality.

We offer a comprehensive 5-year warranty on all Premium packages, 3-year on Standard, and 1-year on Starter packages. This covers structural defects, hardware malfunction, and finish deterioration under normal use conditions.

We offer Marine Plywood, MDF, HDF, High-Pressure Laminates, Acrylic, PVC Boards, Aluminum Frames, and Natural Wood Veneer. Each material is selected based on your specific needs, budget, and the intended application area.
